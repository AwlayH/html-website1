This is a List of All of my Speedrunning Times!

For multiple years myself, along with players from all over the world, attempted to beat raids within Destiny 2 in the fastest time possible, known as speedrunning. We did so under the name "Silimar".
This is a list of just some of the world records I managed to obtain in my time competing! I contribute to a larger number of runs so these are just some of my favourites.
I have since stopped competing due to limitted free time however it remains a very passionate hobby. Here is a link to my profile that contains all of my previous records (they have since been beaten) : Awlay

Raids (6 player activities)
These are activities with complex mechanics usually requiring all 6 player's contribution to progres the level.

  • Crown of Sorrow 9:25 , my first ever world record way back in july of 2020, This took us around a month to get.

  • Garden of Salvation 12:28 , this run actually only took us 4 days and is the fastest time taken to obtain a record in history.

  • Last Wish 17:50 , unlike the previous run, this run actually took the second longest ever time to obtain a record.

Grandmaster Nightfalls (3 player activities)
These are activities with less mechanics and more enemy intensity requriing all three player's teamwork to be perfect.

  • Exodus Crash in 8:17 , my first attempt at a GM world record.

  • Glassway in 14:52 , the hardest nightfall that has ever been in the game.

  • Glassway in 8:51 , another glassway run however my most cherished run.
